Game Rules & Guide

  1. Naruto Challenge

    Objective

    Select the correct ramen brand to help Naruto enjoy his meal!

    Controls

    • Click on Indomie or Sedaap images to make a choice.
    • If you choose the correct ramen, Naruto will enjoy his food!
    • If you choose incorrectly, your attempts will decrease.
    • You have 4 attempts before the game ends.

    Game End

    The game ends when you run out of attempts or win by selecting correctly.

    Strategy Tips

    • Pay attention to clues—Naruto loves his ramen!
    • Stay focused, and don't rush your decisions.
    • Track your score and try to beat your previous record.
  2. Running Zenitsu

    Objective

    Collect as much gold as possible while avoiding enemies. The game continues until zenitsu die, at which point their collected gold is recorded.

    Controls

    • Movement: Use Arrow Keys to move.
    • Running: Hold movement key to enter running state.
    • Gold Collection: Move towards gold to collect it.
    • Avoid Enemies: Stay away to prevent game-over.

    Game End

    The game ends when the character collides with an enemy.

    Strategy Tips

    • Stay Alert - Enemies spawn unpredictably.
    • Prioritize Gold - Aim for the highest gold count.
    • Use Running Wisely - Speed helps but can be risky.
    • Track Your Best Score - Challenge yourself to beat previous records.
  3. Memories Game

    Objective

    Match pairs of memory cards before time runs out!

    Controls

    • Click on a card to flip it.
    • Find the matching card by flipping another one.
    • If the cards match, they remain flipped.
    • If they don't match, they flip back after a short delay.
    • You have limited time to match all the cards.

    Game End

    • You match all card pairs before time runs out (You Win!). Additionally, you will get bonus points from the remaining time.
    • The timer reaches 0 before completing all matches (Game Over).

    Strategy Tips

    • Remember card locations to increase matching speed.
    • Start by flipping different cards to uncover pairs.
    • Try to match quickly to maximize bonus points.
